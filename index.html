Seoul’s Living Heritage: Nature, Museums, and History

Traveling through Seoul, you will find that the city's texture is woven together by mountains, rivers and cultural landmarks.
From Bukaksan Mountain in the north to the museum clusters on both sides of the Han River, nature and history meet here to shape the unique experience of this city.
This map will take you to explore how Seoul's cultural space flows over time and witness the evolution of history.
